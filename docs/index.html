Watermelon Picker

AI-powered watermelon analysis. Take a photo, get visual quality scores, knock sound analysis, and taste predictions to find the sweetest pick.

👁

Visual Analysis

AI examines field spot color, stripe contrast, shape uniformity, and rind texture to score each watermelon's visual quality.

🎵

Knock Sound Analysis

Record a knock on the watermelon. The app analyzes the sound resonance, pitch, and hollowness to gauge ripeness.

🍉

Taste Prediction

Combines visual and sound data to predict sweetness, juiciness, texture, and crunch so you can pick the best one.

🏆

Smart Ranking

When multiple watermelons are in frame, the app ranks them from best to least recommended so you know exactly which to buy.

📷

One-Tap Scan

Just take a photo. The AI automatically detects every watermelon and delivers analysis in seconds. No manual setup needed.

🔒

Privacy First

Photos are analyzed and never stored. No accounts required. Your data stays on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

Take a photo of one or more watermelons. The app uses AI to detect each watermelon, analyze visual markers (field spot, stripes, shape, texture), and predict quality. You can optionally record a knock sound for even more accurate taste predictions.

How many free credits do I get?

Every new user receives 3 free analysis credits. Each credit allows you to analyze one photo. If no watermelons are detected in your photo, the credit is refunded automatically.

How do I get more credits?

You can purchase additional credits directly in the app. We offer a 10-credit pack and a 100-credit pack. Go to Settings > Buy More Credits to purchase.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits never expire and persist on your device even if you reinstall the app.

Do I need an account?

No. Watermelon Picker works without any account, login, or sign-up. Everything is stored locally on your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. Photos are sent to our AI for analysis but are never stored or shared. We do not collect personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What devices are supported?

Watermelon Picker requires an iPhone running iOS 18.0 or later.

Can I restore my purchases?

Credits are consumable purchases tied to your device. You can tap "Restore Purchases" in the purchase screen to process any pending transactions. Note that consumed credits cannot be restored as they are one-time use.
